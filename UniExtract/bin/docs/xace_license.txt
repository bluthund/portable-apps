XAce Plus is  a   small  and   convenient
extractor  for archives created with  Ace
or  WinAce,   a  new  and  widely popular
compression  utility.  If you use Ace  to
compress  the  files  on  your website or
in  your  BBS, provide a link to the Xace
homepage for your users to get the latest
version.

* supports ace/arj/cab/lzh/rar and zip
* multi volumes
* password encrypted files
* Windows Shell Extension
* includes X-Folders
